Warrior Strong is the program for you

You'll follow a structured strength training program that adapts to your weekly progress, challenging you according to your goals and abilities in a personalized, semi-private setting.

  • Customized nutrition
  • Weekly check-ins
  • Monthly measurements
  • Progress tracking
  • All designed to deliver the results you want.

Learn how to build and prioritize lifelong healthy habits, all while enjoying the foods you love and maintaining a balanced lifestyle.

Bia Warrior is built on the belief that true strength comes from within.
Our programs blend physical training with mental resilience, empowering you to overcome any obstacle at any stage in your life.

We help you move your body as it's meant to, developing strength, coordination, and well-being.

As you grow stronger, you'll experience a transformation that goes beyond appearances—it touches every part of your life, forever.

Our community is at the heart of everything we do. We've helped hundreds of women realize their potential, from first-time obstacle course racers to Gov Games champions. You're not just joining a program; you're joining a community that empowers you to achieve more than you ever thought possible. Above all, be celebrated.
