Bia Warrior was born to champion women

We envision a society where every woman and every girl feel confident to take on any physical challenge and chase her dreams, no matter how big or small.

With the right discipline, training, mindset and community, nothing is out of reach.

Supporting today and tomorrow's generation of women

Born in 2019 with a mission

Founded in 2019 by Samia Kallidis and Domia Economides, Bia Warrior is the UAE's first and only all-female obstacle training program, setting a new standard in specialized fitness for women. Their journey began after spending months training together for Gov Games as part of team F3, where they pushed their physical and mental limits. Through this experience, their eyes opened to the lack of support for women in sports and ignited their mission to change that.

Bia Warrior was then born from the belief that every woman has an inner warrior waiting to be unleashed. Samia and Domia wanted to create an empowering space where women could build confidence, overcome fears and break barriers - with the right support, women can achieve anything.

Since starting as a small group of 11, Bia Warrior has grown into a community of over 500 members and a team of expert passionate trainers, coaching Gov Games winning teams in 2021 and 2023, and securing a podium finish in 2022. Their programs, designed for women at all fitness levels, include Obstacle Training, Warrior Strong, Bia Mama, Bia Girls, Gov Games training, and online coaching.

Bia Warrior blends physical fitness with mental and emotional well-being through mindful morning sessions, retreats, and transformative programs, redefining strength and community.
