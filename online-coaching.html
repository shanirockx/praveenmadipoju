Customized to suit your lifestyle

Our online coaching program helps you achieve your goals by simplifying nutrition and creating a sustainable fitness routine that suits your lifestyle. Become the strongest,and most confident version of yourself, at every stage of your life.

Onboarding consultation call

Once you sign up, we kick off with a detailed consultation call to understand your lifestyle habits and fitness goals.

Health and lifestyle questionnaire

Then, you will complete an in-depth health and lifestyle questionnaire, which combined with our consultation call, helps us create your bespoke online training and nutrition program to help you succeed.

In-depth welcome video

Once we've created your plan, we'll send you an in-depth video that breaks down your personalized program and guides you step-by-step, ensuring you know exactly what to do to achieve your results.

Custom training & nutrition plan

Bye guesswork, hello guided plans. Your plan will specify your daily calories, protein, carbohydrates and fats you should be having based on your goals. You'll know what foods to prioritise, and how to build lifelong healthy balanced eating habits while still enjoying the foods you love.

Accountability with weekly check-ins

Building a healthy lifestyle is more than just your hour in the gym. We'll check-in on your energy, hunger, recovery, steps, water intake, and sleep - all the things that will affect your health, fitness performance and results. With weekly feedback, we'll celebrate wins and identify areas for improvement, keeping you motivated and on track.

Unlimited coach and community support

Get ongoing support and encouragement from the Bia Warrior community throughout your fitness journey! Regular communication and feedback will help you stay on track and ensure results as long as you follow the plan. You'll get your very own full-time coach to support, motivate and guide you through your journey.

Benefits of an online coach

1

Save Time

Get an online program you can do from anywhere without missing out on actual coaching to ensure that you're doing what your body needs, while staying injury free.

2

Get a Plan

Set routines so habits become part of your daily schedule with a realistic approach to make it a lifestyle, built around your family and work so they'll stick for life.

3

Accountability

Working with a coach increases your chances of reaching your goals by 95%. Accountability makes all the difference -if you're finally ready to reach your goals.

4

Get Results

Receive personalized, effective workouts and expert feedback that suit your level, ensuring you're never lost in what to do or how.
