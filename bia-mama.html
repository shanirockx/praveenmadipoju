Bia Mama

Feel strong and functional throughout your pregnancy and postpartum recovery.

Not sure where to start, what to do, or how?

PERSONALIZED PLAN

A unique program built for your needs

Get the confidence to workout with a program that evolves with your body's needs, ensuring safety and results at every stage of your pregnancy and postpartum journey.

With 2-3 weekly semi-private training sessions, limited to 6 women at a ladies-only studio, you'll receive personalized attention from certified pre- and postnatal coaches.

The program is designed to strengthen your core, improve mobility and support your pelvic floor, helping you feel empowered and prepared through every phase of pregnancy and postpartum recovery.

You'll get nutritional coaching to ensure you're nourishing yourself in the best way possible to support your needs.

Pregnancy and Postpartum Support

Joining Bia Mama gives you access to expert coaching, a support network and a range of resources that make navigating motherhood and your fitness journey easier.

Personalized program, semi-private setting

Our certified trainers create a tailored program to guide you through different phases of your pregnancy and postpartum recovery.

Certified pre and postnatal coaches

Guidance from experienced coaches who have specialized certifications and firsthand knowledge of the challenges of pregnancy and postpartum recovery.

Nourishing nutrition support

Receive intuitive eating strategies to nourish your body throughout each trimester and beyond, so you can feel at home in your evolving body.

Specialized functional sessions

Focus on strength training, core and pelvic floor health, movement and mobility to help you regain function, move pain-free and feel strong.

Specialist support network

Access our network of pelvic floor physical therapists who can help you prevent and address common pregnancy and postpartum challenges.

Supportive mama community

Benefit and connect with a mom community that have been through the same journey, offering advice and encouragement at every step.
