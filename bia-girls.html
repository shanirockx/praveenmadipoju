Bia Girls

Empowering the next generation of warriors through obstacle training and community

Essential physical and mental skills to help 9-14 year girls thrive in life and on the course

SUPPORTIVE COMMUNITY

Building confidence and friendship among girls

Every girl deserves the opportunity to discover her inner strength and resilience, alongside a community that celebrates her journey.

Led by expert female coaches, the program is designed to help 9-14 year old girls build both physical fitness and self-confidence, while creating lasting friendships to thrive in all aspects of their lives.

Young girls deserve more support

Research shows that 45% of girls drop out of youth sports by the age of 14, a rate 40% higher than boys. The Bia Girls program seeks to combat these statistics by instilling a love for physical activity and a sense of achievement from a young age.

Structured learning curriculum

Our progressive strength program builds a strong foundation, allowing your girl to learn new skills safely. She will also be introduced to new movements that encourage creativity and problem solving.

Highly qualified female coaches

Our highly experienced team of female coaches have been through it all -they will provide guidance, support and encouragement that helps your daughter believe she can do anything.

Teamwork and problem solving

Through physical and mental group challenges, your girl will develop teamwork, communication, and problem solving skills, through a series of physical and mental group challenges.

Personalized individual feeback

Your girl will get a dedicated coach who will guide her journey throughout the term, providing personalized and individual feedback to support her in reaching her goals.

Limited class capacity

We limit our class sizes to ensure that every girl gets the personalized attention that they deserve, to support their growth and safety in every session.

Movement skill development

Your girl will learn how to move safely, by learning how to fall first. This allows her to safely make “mistakes” and learn to move well and with confidence - up, under, over and around any obstacle.

Bia Girls is for you if you want

1

Strength + Skills

Unlike any other youth program, Bia Girls provides a comprehensive curriculum - including strength training, skills training, and problem solving activities to help girls thrive.

2

Confidence

The sense of achievement your girl will feel overcoming a new obstacle in every class will tremendously boost her confidence and builds perseverance.

3

Support

Getting the support of female mentors who lead by example is crucial in shaping young girls' minds. Our coaches offer a safe judgement-free space that encourages your girl to be herself..

4

Mental Resilience

Obstacle training improves focus, determination, cognitive, and problem solving skills. By overcoming her fears, your girl will learn that no challenge is too great to tackle.

5

Friendship

Being part of a supportive team of girls and having a consistent coach helps your girl foster meaningful connections and a sense of belonging.

A safe space where young girls can feel supported, free of judgement.

Every girl deserves to feel strong, confident, and supported. At Bia Girls, your daughter will be surrounded by supportive mentors and peers who encourage her to challenge herself, grow stronger, learn new skills and believe in her ability to overcome any obstacle in her path.
