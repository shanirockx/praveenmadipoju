Zanzibar

October 14-21, 2024

35 50 10
Days Hours Minutes

De-stress, recharge and reconnect

We've brought together fitness, wellness, food and fun, for you to spend time with like-minded women on the most beautiful, carefree and zen island.

We are lovers of adventure, the ocean, quiet time, and relaxation - all of which is in this retreat.

In order to look after ourselves, we need to spend time on ourselves. We believe in working hard and striving for your goals, but also in setting boundaries and taking time to reset. This retreat offers you the perfect balance of action and relaxation, in order to look inward, reflect, and recharge.

What's included

  • Return flights + Airport Transfers
    DXB to Zanzibar
  • 6 Night Luxury stay in a shared Deluxe room with 2 meals per day
  • Daily workout & stretch sessions
  • Daily Mindful Mornings with journaling and breathwork
  • Private lunch on a beautiful sandbank in the middle of the ocean
  • National Forest hike to see some furry local friends
  • 2 snorkeling / diving trips
  • Giant turtle sanctuary visit
  • Spice market & Stone Town visit
  • Exclusive warrior welcome pack
  • Sunset dhow cruise
